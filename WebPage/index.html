Sneakers have rose to the top and have became more mainstream these past couple years. Limited edition Sneakers like Jordan 3s, Jordan 1s, Nike Dunks, etc are being resold for a profit since there is a limited quantity most people get numerous pairs and sell it at a greater price to make money. Reselling shoes has always been a thing, it isn't new ,but it has gained a rise in popularity in recent years. Many shoes that were "sitting" in stores because the colorway wasnt popular,are now going for 2x+ the price a couple years later.
